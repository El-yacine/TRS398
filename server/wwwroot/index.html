TRS-398 Formulas

• Ktp = (1013/P) × (273.2 + T) / 293.2
• Kpol = (|Mean M+| + |Mean M-|) / (2 × |Mean M+|) ← polarity correction
• Ks = 1.198 - (0.875 × Mean M+/Mean 100V) + (0.677 × (Mean M+/Mean 100V)²)
• M_corr = Mean M+ × Ktp × Kpol × Ks
• DW,Zref = M_corr × kQ × Nd,w
• Ecart (%) = 100 × (1 - DW,Zref)
